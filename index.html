The Gap

Potential alone is never enough.

Extraordinary potential without direction. Achievement without fulfillment. Influence without integrity. Leadership without stewardship. Organizations that grow in size while drifting away from the values that once defined them.

We don't see these as failures of knowledge. We see them as failures of formation.

Knowledge informs. Formation transforms.

Who We Are

A human development institution.

RERWA is a human development institution that builds the capacity individuals and organizations need to live meaningful lives and create lasting impact — through character formation, leadership development, strategic advisory, coaching, retreats, and research.

Our Approach

How we think about capacity.

At RERWA, we understand capacity as something built across four connected domains — individual, professional, organizational, and societal — through a continuous cycle of research, learning, application, and reflection, not a single event.
